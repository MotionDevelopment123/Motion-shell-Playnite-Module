FFPLAY
Launch video
Launch video size
Playnite

C:\Playnote-Shell\FFPLAY.LOCATION
C:\Playnote-Shell\VIDEO.LOCATION
C:\Playnote-Shell\SCREENSIZE.PIXELS
C:\Playnote-Shell\PLAYNITE.LOCATION
